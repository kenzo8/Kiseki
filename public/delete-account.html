Delete your account – Kien

This page explains how to request that your Kien app account and associated data are deleted. Kien is the app name shown on the store listing.

Steps to request account and data deletion

  1. Open the Kien app on your device.
  2. Sign in to your account if you are not already signed in.
  3. Go to Settings (e.g. from the profile or menu).
  4. Find the Account section and tap Delete Account.
  5. In the dialog, confirm your email and read the notice about permanent deletion.
  6. Tap Delete Account to confirm. Your account and all associated data will be permanently removed.

Deletion is performed immediately from within the app. There is no separate web form to submit; use the in-app flow above.

Data that is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered:

  • Your account (authentication identity)
  • Your profile (display name, photo, and other profile fields)
  • All device entries (seki) linked to your account
  • All wishlist entries (wants) linked to your account

Data retention

No additional retention. After you confirm deletion in the app, we do not retain your account or the data listed above. Deletion is permanent with no retention period.

If you have trouble deleting your account in the app, you can contact the developer (see the app’s store listing or in-app feedback/support).
