Request data deletion – Kien

This page explains how to request that some or all of your data in the Kien app is deleted. Kien is the app name shown on the store listing. You can delete data without deleting your account.

Steps to request that your data is deleted

  1. Open the Kien app on your device and sign in.
  2. To delete a device entry: Open the device (from your profile or explore), then use Delete or Remove to remove it from your profile. That device’s data is permanently deleted.
  3. To remove a wishlist item: Open the device from your wants list and choose to remove it from your wants. That wishlist entry is permanently deleted.
  4. Repeat as needed to delete as many devices or wishlist items as you want. Your account stays active.
  5. To delete all of your data and your account: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account and confirm. See our account deletion page for details.

Types of data that are deleted or kept

When you delete a device or remove a wishlist item (without deleting your account):

  • Deleted: That device entry (name, notes, and any linked data) or that wishlist entry. Deletion is permanent.
  • Kept: Your account, profile (display name, photo), and all other device and wishlist data you did not delete.

When you delete your account: Your account, profile, and all device and wishlist data are permanently deleted. See delete-account.html for full details.

Retention

No additional retention. When you delete a device, remove a wishlist item, or delete your account, we do not retain that data. Deletion is permanent with no retention period.
